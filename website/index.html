About Cardly

A powerful flashcard application built with Flutter and Firebase to help you learn and memorize any topic effectively.

Using the proven SuperMemo 2 (SM-2) spaced repetition algorithm, Cardly ensures you review cards at optimal intervals for maximum retention. Whether you're learning languages, preparing for exams, or mastering new skills, Cardly adapts to your learning pace.
